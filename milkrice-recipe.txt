Ingrediants
Red raw rice 500g
coconut milk 1 liter
Water
Salt 10g

Instructions
Wash the rice accordingly.

Add coconut milk, 100ml of water, salt & rice to a small pot.

Keep the pot in a stove and provide heat. Once the liquids are absorbed by the rice & cooked pour it to a dish and flatten. Leave 10 mins for chill, then cut 2'x2' piecs and serve.

You can use white rice, if you don't have red raw rice.
